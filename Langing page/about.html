About UPSEA

Since the 1980s, the founding members of UPSEA have been committed to nurturing equestrian sports in Uttar Pradesh. From hosting National and International Championships to organizing the annual U.P. Horse Show since 2012, UPSEA has been at the forefront of promoting horse riding and equestrian excellence.

In 2021, UPSEA was officially registered as the State Equestrian Association, strengthening its mission to develop riders, support breeders, and bridge traditional horse sports with modern professional equestrianism.

Disciplines

UPSEA proudly promotes a diverse range of equestrian disciplines, including Tent Pegging, Endurance, Dressage, Show Jumping, Eventing, Polo, Gymkhana, Hacks, Indigenous Sports, and Horseback Archery.

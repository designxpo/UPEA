What we do

UPSEA is dedicated to the development and promotion of horse sports, training of riders, competitions across Uttar Pradesh, supporting riders and horse breeders, and promoting excellence in equestrianism.

State Equestrian Championship

UPSEA conducts an Annual State Championship, promoting excellence in equestrian sports.

Inter-School Competitions

We organize Inter-School Equestrian Events to inspire young talent and build the next generation of riders.

Arena Polo Events

UPSEA promotes the thrill of Arena Polo, offering a dynamic and spectator-friendly version of polo.

Training & Development

Through seminars, workshops, and clinics, we support the growth of riders, coaches, trainers, and grooms.
